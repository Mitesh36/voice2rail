Need Help!

1.Complaint Form

    Step1: Go to the Complaint Form by Clicking on Register Complaint Menu.
    Step2: Enter your all valid details about your self and also about journey.
    Step3: Provide all details about the station where you found the problems.
    Step4: After Submitting all details properly you will get complaint number, by using that complaint number you can track the progress of your complaint.

2.Suggestion Form

Step1: Go to the Suggestion Form by Clicking on Suggestion Menu.
Step2: Enter your all valid details about your self and also about journey.
Step3: Provide all details about the station where you want to give suggestion as well as you think we did our work appropriately than you can give us words with appreciation.
Step4: After Submitting all details properly you will redirecting to homepage.

3.Track Complaint

Step1: Go to the Track Complaint Form by Clicking on Track Complaint Menu.
Step2: By Providing your Complaint Number that you got at the time of Register Complaint. You will get Status of your Complaint.

Note: Click on the linked heading Text or arrows on the right side to expand or collapse accordion panels.
